emily mariko's salmon rice bowl

description

the viral tiktok recipe

emily mariko is a 29-year-old japanese american influencer. she went viral on tiktok for her salmon rice bowl video, which many went on to recreate.

this dish is like a chirashi bowl. mariko's version of the dish was a way to eat leftovers: salmon and rice. the delicious recipe is quite simple to make! it is also customizable!

ingredients

makes one serving

  • leftover salmon
  • leftover rice
  • 1 ice cube
  • soy sauce
  • kewpie
  • sriracha
  • kimchi
  • seaweed

steps

  1. crush salmon
  2. put rice and an ice cube on top of salmon; microwave with cover
  3. drizzle soy sauce, kewpie, and sriracha on plate
  4. mix everything on plate
  5. eat with seaweed and kimchi

and you're done! feel free to try out different toppings and sides!

poke bowl

description

a classic hawaiian dish

poke is a hawaiian food made with seasoned fresh raw fish. this recipe is for a poke bowl, which is a twist on poke with rice and other ingredients.

poke is very customizeable, you can swap out toppings with pretty much anything. poke bowls often contain a balance of nutrients to nourish you to the fullest. in the end, you will have a tasty bowl of nutrition!

this recipe is adapted from this one.

ingredients

makes two servings

for tuna:

  • 1/2 lb sushi grade tuna
  • 1/2 cup scallions
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp sriracha

for spicy mayo:

  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise or kewpie
  • 2 tsp sriracha

for bowl:

  • 1 cup cooked short-grain rice
  • 1 cup cucumbers
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 2 scallions
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • soy sauce
  • sriracha

steps

  1. cut tuna into 1/2 in cubes
  2. mix ingredients for tuna
  3. mix spicy mayo ingredients
  4. mix rice vinegar and cooked rice to make sushi rice
  5. peel and dice cucumber into 1/2 in cubes
  6. slice avocado and scallions
  7. add everything to the bowl
  8. drizzle soy sauce and sriracha on top

and there you have it! a poke bowl! hope you enjoy it and remember that you can add essentially any toppings/seasonings you want!

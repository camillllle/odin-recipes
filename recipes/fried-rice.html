fried rice

description

a traditional take on the popular dish

fried rice is a widespread dish. however, almost every recipe i see is very americanized. it always tastes good, but it is very different from authentic fried rice.

this dish is an authentic take on fried rice. it is pretty different from the fried rice many know, just to warn you. it doesn't use soy sauce or sesame oil, and the toppings are pretty different from the ones you can get at panda express.

this recipe is partially adapted from this one.

ingredients

3 servings

  • 3 cups leftover rice
  • 3 chinese sausages
  • 2 scallions
  • 2 eggs
  • cooking oil of your choice
  • salt

steps

  1. slice sausages and scallions
  2. beat eggs
  3. heat oil on wok/skillet on medium-high heat, add beaten eggs. break apart into smaller pieces as they cook
  4. set eggs aside once fully cooked
  5. set wok/skillet on high heat, add oil and sausage. cook 1 minute while stirring
  6. add rice and stir so no clumps remain
  7. add eggs and scallions back and stir more
  8. serve!

you're done! hope you enjoy!
